This quote is commonly (mis)attributed to Albert Einstein, however, no one seems to be able to find any evidence of Uncle Al ever saying (or writing it). Cherne may have said it, but even that is hard to track down. It has also been attributed to a guy named Stuart Walesh, but that wasn't until the 1980s.

Ben Shoemate did some extensive research into this, but was unable to solve the mystery of the exact origin of this quote. So who really said it first?
